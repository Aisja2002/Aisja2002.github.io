Aisja: wherever music will take

Aisja (Aisja Baglioni) was born in 2002 in Terni, Italy. She has graduated in modern singing with VES® in 2020, and is now pursuing a career as a vocal teacher and singer-songwriter. She began producing her own tracks in 2023, creating a colorful and narrative-rich musical world that's always evolving, inspired by medias such as novels and videogames. She loves experimenting through different genres, but is mostly influenced by soundtracks, alternative rock and asian pop, distinguishing herself for the capacity to switch between moods and styles. Being active in the Italian poetry community, she won several writing awards since 2019. Formerly a swing singer for her town's orchestra and frontwoman for a rock band until 2020. Additionally, she is now part of the progressive metal band Igarka, and is involved in an idol project under the pseudonym Tori.

Tori: the blue-rose idol

Meet Tori! Tori is an eclectic voice and a porcelain girl, who makes music a megaphone for the most fragile and hidden emotions. Don't be fooled by her shy and awkward appearance, on stage her soul is rock. Her aim is to capture every colour of the world with her voice, to make it a canvas for all spectators, so that her listeners can find a hidden truth with her and, in the time of a song, give it form.

Igarka: is progressive metal still a thing?

Igarka is a band formed in Rimini in 2023 that experiments with alternative and progressive metal sounds. They unite under the name of a distant city in Siberia, as if to signify that there is no outside push in their career. With 7 songs under their belt and as many more ready to be composed, Igarka are carving a place for themselves in the international music scene and aspire to make their music known in Europe and beyond.

VES: beauty in teaching

Aisja Baglioni is a singer-songwriter and singing teacher, graduated in Modern Singing with honours and Certified VES® Professional Singer at VES - Voice Evolution Institute. Now attending the degree course in Information Engineering at the University of Bologna - Cesena Campus. Since 2018 she has been vocalist, percussionist and keyboard player in the City of Rimini Youth Band, and singer of small ensembles, with concerts to her credit in Rimini, San Marino and the surrounding area. A collaborator of the association Rinascita Culturale RSM - San Marino, she curates projects and literary competitions in the field of poetry, also participating in anthologies and writing workshops. She has been teaching with the VES system since 2023 as a Candidate VES Trainer, and with her it is possible to obtain the VES Diploma in Modern Singing.
